LAYERS OF ATMOSPHERE

Troposphere:

0 to 12 Km-This is where weather conditions occur. The wind and most clouds exists in this layer.

Stratosphere:

12 to 50Km-The lower part of the stratosphere contains the ozone layer.Ozone acts as a shield for the earth's surface. It absorbs ultraviolet radiation from the sun.Many aircraft fly in this layer.

Mesosphere:

50 to 80 km-Meteors burn up in this layer

Thermosphere:

80km and above- The temperature is very high inthis layer,often reaching 2000 degree celsius. The air is very thin. this layer includes ionosphere and exosphere.

Ionosphere:

ionosphere is the lower part othis thermosphere.Radioo waves are reflected back to the earth from this layer. This helps in radio communication. This area where satellites orbit thge earth.

Exosphere:

exosphere is the upper part of the thermosphere. Air is very thin here.
